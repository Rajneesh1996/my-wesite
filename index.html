Cardiology Department

Comprehensive heart care including prevention, diagnosis and treatment.

Our cardiology unit provides ECG, Echo, TMT and cardiac consultation services for conditions such as hypertension, heart disease and chest pain evaluation. Experienced cardiologists ensure accurate diagnosis and preventive care.

Neurology Department

Diagnosis and treatment of brain, spine and nerve disorders.

Our neurology specialists manage conditions such as migraine, epilepsy, stroke, neuropathy and spinal disorders with modern diagnostic support and personalized treatment plans.

Gastroenterology Department

Specialized care for digestive and liver disorders.

Diagnosis and treatment of acidity, ulcers, liver disease, abdominal pain and digestive disorders using advanced medical evaluation and treatment protocols.

Pediatrics Department

Complete healthcare for infants, children and adolescents.

Child health services including vaccination, growth monitoring, nutrition guidance and treatment of childhood illnesses by experienced pediatricians.

Ophthalmology Department

Diagnosis and treatment of eye and vision disorders.

Eye examinations, vision correction, cataract evaluation and treatment of common eye conditions using modern ophthalmic equipment and specialist care.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common patient queries regarding appointments, treatment and hospital services.

How can I book an appointment with a doctor?

You can book an appointment online through our website, call our reception number, or visit the hospital directly. Our team will confirm your schedule with the selected doctor.

Do you provide 24/7 emergency services?

Yes, Medilife Care Hospital operates a 24-hour emergency unit with trained medical staff, ambulance support and critical care facilities available at all times.

What medical departments are available?

Our hospital provides services in General Medicine, Cardiology, Neurology, Pediatrics, Orthopedics, Gynecology, Diagnostics and other specialty departments.

Do you have diagnostic and laboratory facilities?

Yes, we offer in-house pathology tests, health checkups and diagnostic services to ensure accurate and timely medical evaluation for patients.

Is cashless insurance or mediclaim accepted?

We support multiple health insurance providers and cashless hospitalization facilities. Please contact our billing desk for insurance verification and assistance.

Where is Medilife Care Hospital located?

Medilife Care Hospital is located in Nangloi, New Delhi, with easy road access and nearby public transport connectivity.
